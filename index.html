About SCOPE

The School of Computer Science and Engineering (SCOPE) at VIT Vellore is one of the largest and most prestigious schools in the university. Our vision is to provide quality teaching and impactful research that meets global standards. We focus on cutting-edge areas like AI, Cybersecurity, and Data Science to prepare students for the evolving IT industry.

B.Tech CSE (AI & Machine Learning)

M.Tech Software Engineering

Advanced study in software methodologies and enterprise systems.
